About Us

Vishnu is one of the 3 high gods in the Hindu pantheon. Vishnu is the sustainer who self-incarnates in order to save the universe by purging evil & restoring cosmic order.

Whenever righteousness wanes and unrighteousness increases I send myself forth. For the protection of the good and for the destruction of evil, and for the establishment of righteousness, I come into being age after age.
- Bhagavad Gita 4.7-8

On the ancient battlefields of India, warriors blew their horns to fire them up before they charged into battle.

This is where the name Vishnu's Trumpet (aka the vt) comes from.

The vt was built by a group of artists who were dissatisfied with the existing digital ecosystem & so built their own streaming service & artist-driven network. We are driven to build a better world for artists everywhere, as well as all beings across the universe.

We have the only functional financial model in the music industry, built by & for artists. When we pay artists, we end up paying them more than all streaming services, combined. This is very enjoyable, and we are always working to increase our artist payments.

We are also actively building our own artist promotion network as part of an artist collective.

Do you love discovering new music & artists, but feel totally overwhelmed by the onslaught of content online today?

We've created a simple, fun & intuitive new music discovery service for you.

Each week we feature a new artist with an amazing album we think you'll enjoy listening to. Our community-curated release schedule is full of wonderful talent from a wide variety of genres. A lot of psych rock and underground hip hop, for sure, but also indian classical jazz fusion and arthouse EDM and all kinds of crazy junk in between. We like finding music & artists who create sounds & craft music that is evocative of our world today & helps us cope. Positive vibes, positive sounds, positive minds.
